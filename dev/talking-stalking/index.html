PETITION

Stalking is designated as a bailable offence in Indian criminal law, which belies the seriousness of the offence and puts victims at further risk.

The Quint has collaborated with senior advocate and veteran activist Kamini Jaiswal to prepare a petition to MPs and the government to make stalking a non-bailable offence, which will help ensure that stalking is treated with the gravity it requires and ensures judicial scrutiny before perpetrators are allowed to roam free.
