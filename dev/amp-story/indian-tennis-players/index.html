Indian Tennis Players to Watch at Asian Games

The Quint Lab

18.07.2018

With experienced players Yuki Bhambri and Sania Mirza missing, here are six players to watch out for in the Indian Asian Games tennis squad.

Leander Paes

Eight-time Asian Games medallist Paes will be taking part in his first Asiad in 12 years.

Ramkumar Ramanathan

With Yuki Bhambri unavailable, Ramanathan will take the lead in singles.

Rohan Bopanna

Bopanna & Paes beat Mo Xin Gong & Ze Zhang in the David Cup doubles match vs China.

Divij Sharan

He clinched the bronze medal in the doubles draw with Bhambri in Incheon in 2014.

Ankita Raina

Raina became the 5th Indian woman to break into the top 200 in the rankings in April this year.

Karman Kaur Thandi

Thandi is yet to play a Grand Slam. Has played for India just once, in the 2018 Fed Cup.
