What Sanju Didn't Show About Sanjay Dutt's Life

The Quint Lab

05.07.2018

Where's the Dhak Dhak girl?

There's no mention of the alleged Sanjay Dutt-Madhuri Dixit affair.

No 'rocky marriage' references

Sanjay's first wife, Richa Sharma, died of brain tumor in 1996.

Baba divorced Rhea Pillai in 2005, after seven years of marriage.

Skips the daughter

Sanjay Dutt's eldest daughter, Trishala, lives in the USA. The film has no reference to her.

Fires the firing episode

In 1982, Sanjay reportedly got drunk and opened fire near his Pali Hill house.

Sidelines the sisters

Sanjay's sisters Namrata and Priya stood by him throughout. They were virtually invisible in the movie.
