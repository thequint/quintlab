The Gujarat Elections 2017 bugle has been sounded, and the key political contenders in the fray have left no stone unturned in preparation. Rahul Gandhi and Prime Minister Narendra Modi have carried out back-to-back rallies in Gujarat, grasping at every opportunity for potshots at the other.

The Gujarat polls have prompted shifting alliances, with the Hardik Patel-led PAAS putting its weight behind the Congress. The Gujarat election schedule is packed, with the first phase of voting on 9 December, and the second on 14 December.

The result will be declared on 18 December. While the Election Commission has disallowed any exit poll predictions before 14 December, most opinion polls have predicted a BJP victory. Which way will this Gujarat chunav go? Follow The Quint for any and all Gujarat election news.
